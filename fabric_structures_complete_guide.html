🌍 Why Sustainable Fibers Matter for Decarbonisation

The global textile industry emits ~1.2 billion tonnes of CO₂ equivalent per year — more than aviation and shipping combined. Conventional cotton uses 10,000 litres of water per kg; synthetic fibers release microplastics; and fast fashion sends 92 million tonnes to landfill annually. Switching to sustainable fibers across woven and knitted structures is one of the highest-impact interventions available to brands and manufacturers.
